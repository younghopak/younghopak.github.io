WELCOME TO AHS

Who We Are

AHS has grown from the vision of a Canadian psychologist who recognized the need for high quality, effective psychological counseling services in Korea. Starting as a one-woman company, AHS has continually grown in size but maintained an unwavering dedication to professionalism, confidentiality and measurable outcomes.

There is no question that Korean culture is vastly different from those cultures which many expats in Korea come from, and it may be difficult to find someone who can really understand your experience.

To that end, our English therapy team is composed of many Western expatriates who were born, raised and trained in the West...

Read more

What We Do

We work in partnership with our clients to support them in meeting their goals, overcoming difficult obstacles, and increasing their positive experiences in Korea.

All of our therapists understand the many benefits and difficulties that come with a “global citizenship”. We care deeply about promoting the best possible experience for members of our community, and in providing a safety net for our clients to use as they navigate their life in Korea.

A Good Fit Makes All the Difference

Who We Serve

Our clients are accomplished people and organizations who know the benefits of investing in themselves and their loved ones. To date our clients represent over 50 countries and include individual expats & locals, their families, globally-minded Koreans, local companies and top 50 multi-national corporations.

Therapy Services

Good Fit Matters

At AHS we ensure that you are linked with the therapist who will best meet your needs and preferences. With a diverse group of licensed therapists, we are able to provide you with native English, native Korean or Bilingual speaking counselors who have varying backgrounds, specialties, and styles to support you in effectively meeting your therapeutic goals.

Core Philosophies

  • Asking for help is a sign of sanity, perhaps even wisdom.
  • Being heard, seen, and understood is a basic human need.
  • Everyone deserves the chance to hope, grow, and enjoy relief from pain
  • Transformation can be daunting, but is very rarely impossible.

Inclusive Services

At AHS we hold to the belief that therapy should be culturally relevant and open to anyone who is seeking care, change, and a place to process the complexities that come with being human. We care deeply about creating space for people and we hold to the highest ethical standards of inclusive work.
